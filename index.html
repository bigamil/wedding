Our love story

First meet

Nov 1 2016

Andrew had just moved RideController out of his garage and into the back of Tanner's Customs in early 2016. One day a beautiful, young lady pulled in a massive LIMO that needed body work. Andrew thought to himself, "who is that!?" So the story began.

First date

JUL 17 2018

After over a year of being friends, Andrew finally wised up and asked Stephanie out! Our first date was here at Westside Locale. Andrew was super excited because he always wanted a woman he thought was "cool" and Stephanie was def "cool" and so much more.

Proposal

JUL 5 2020

We frequent Blue Springs lake for fishing, relaxing, and sporting. It has also hosted many date nights for us, as we got to know each other better. So what seemed to be one of our typical relaxing “lake date nights” turned out to be Andrew asking me to be his wife. Andrew staged it all as me teaching him to fly the drone comfortably over the water. I was walking him threw all the maneuvers of the drone and he said, “does this mean it is recording.” Next thing you know he is down on one knee and if you have seen the video, you can see I could not even believe it was happening. I blackout for moment, came back to it, and could not stop shaking my head yes while fighting off tears.
